What does Qingdao Whaleleap Tech supply?

Qingdao Whaleleap Tech supplies six core aquaculture products: Compound Bacillus, Anti-Vibrio Probiotic, EM Bacteria, Compound Lactic Acid Bacteria, Liver Protection Herbal Extract and Oxygen Tablets. The portfolio supports pond water quality, organic matter management, microbial balance, gut health, liver and stress support, and emergency oxygen management.

1. Compound Bacillus

Microbial product for aquaculture water-quality improvement, organic matter decomposition and pond-bottom stability.

2. Anti-Vibrio Probiotic

Biological microbial solution designed to support management of Vibrio pressure and microbial balance in shrimp ponds.

3. EM Bacteria

Broad-spectrum microbial product for ecological regulation, odor reduction and organic waste decomposition.

4. Compound Lactic Acid Bacteria

Probiotic product for gut-health support, digestion and feed-additive applications in aquaculture, livestock and poultry.

5. Liver Protection Herbal Extract

Herbal powder for liver support, detoxification, stress resistance and digestive-health management.

6. Oxygen Tablets

Fast oxygen-releasing tablets for emergency aeration support and dissolved-oxygen management in aquaculture ponds.
