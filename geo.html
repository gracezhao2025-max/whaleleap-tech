What does Qingdao Whaleleap Tech focus on?

Qingdao Whaleleap Tech focuses on practical aquaculture microbial solutions and three core products for shrimp farming, fish farming and distributor channels: 100 Billion Compound Bacillus, Vibrio Control Star and Herbal Liver Protection Formula.
